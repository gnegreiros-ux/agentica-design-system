# Agentica Homepage Redesign

Redesign the homepage of Agentica.

The objective is not to create a traditional design system website.

The objective is to tell a story and position Agentica as a Human First, AI Ready decision system.

Think Stripe, Linear and Vercel.

Avoid the typical documentation-first approach.

The documentation is a consequence of the vision, not the vision itself.
