# Agentica: the systemic memory of digital decision-making

Agentica is a decision system designed for humans and AI agents, turning choices about UX, UI, accessibility, and governance into structured, durable knowledge.
This system rests on a "single source of truth" architecture where humans retain decision-making and governance power, while AI agents handle detection, analysis, and automation.

## Here are Agentica's benefits grouped by persona:

### The Organization
For the organization, Agentica acts as a shared memory of decisions, making knowledge both legible and governable.
It turns knowledge into a strategic asset that survives staff turnover and technology cycles, protecting the institutional investment.
By centralizing decisions that are often scattered, the system curbs the accumulation of UX debt and reduces the impact of technical obsolescence.

### Managers
Managers benefit from visible, traceable trade-offs, where every choice is directly tied to business value and project constraints.
Since every decision carries a "memory" (context, alternatives, and trade-offs), the system keeps teams from reinventing solutions to already-solved problems, ensuring better operational continuity.

### Designers
Agentica lets designers preserve their design intent beyond simple visual mockups.
Thanks to specialized agents able to generate technical specifications and components, designers can focus on high-level creation while AI executes repetitive tasks.
The system ensures the reasoning behind every accessibility rule or color choice stays intact and documented.

### Developers
For developers, the system provides explicit contracts that let them build without having to interpret or guess original intent. By relying on open W3C standards and native Web Components, Agentica offers full portability: decisions (ADRs) are instantly usable in any framework (React, Angular, Vue) with no technology lock-in.
Specialized agents also help implement compliant, readable code.
